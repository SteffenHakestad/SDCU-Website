CASE HISTORY 1

Case study for North Sea Operator on Norwegian Platform using 2x Dynamic Desanders including associated equipment such as remote choke, flor back iron and ESD

Background

Production test performed showed that the well had no productivity/injectivity and is therefore completely plugged.

Clean up needed to continue completion of well.

Solution

2 x Dynamic Desanders including associated equipment such as remote choke, flow back iron and ESD.

Cooperated with Coiled Tubing (CT).

Challenges

Large amount of scale and solids.

Results

Significantly reduced the amount of sand and scale in well.

Total of 11 813 kg of solids were recovered.

Well was treated continuously and separated sand without any interruption of well flow.

Completed the operation with no HSE incidents
