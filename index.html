ABOUT US

INCREASE YOUR PRODUCTION

If sand, solids, or particulate production is impairing your true production capabilities, then the novel technologies and game changing solutions can enable you to increase your production, whilst neutralizing your sand, solids, or particulate production challenges at surface

Our Innovative Dynamic Desander System (DDSTM) requires minimal space, has low rigging/installation time and POB. We will take your Desanding challenges to the next level with our semi or fully autonomous units.
